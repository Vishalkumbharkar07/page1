Shopping Cart Discount Calculator

A supermarket offers discount based on the total purchase amount and the loyalty card status.write a program that calculate the final ammount a customer needs to pay:

  • If the total purchase amount is less than $50,no discount is applied.
  • If the total purchase amount is between $50 and $100,apply a 10% discount.
  • If the total purchase amount is more than $100:
    • Apply a 15% discount if the customer has gold loyalty card.
    • Apply a 12% discount if the customer has a silver loyalty card.
    • Apply a 5% discount if the customer does not have any loyalty card
